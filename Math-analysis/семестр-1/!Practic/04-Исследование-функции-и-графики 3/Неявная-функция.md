\Large

Дано: $\Large f = \ln(u^3 + v^2)$, где $\Large u = xy$, $\Large v = \frac{x}{y}$

Найдём частные производные $\Large \frac{\partial f}{\partial x}$ и $\Large \frac{\partial f}{\partial y}$.

---

**Частная производная по $\Large x$:**

$$\Large \frac{\partial f}{\partial x} = \frac{\partial}{\partial x}\ln(u^3 + v^2)$$

Используем цепное правило:

$$\Large \frac{\partial f}{\partial x} = \frac{1}{u^3 + v^2} \cdot \frac{\partial}{\partial x}(u^3 + v^2)$$

$$\Large = \frac{1}{u^3 + v^2} \cdot \left(3u^2 \frac{\partial u}{\partial x} + 2v \frac{\partial v}{\partial x}\right)$$

Теперь найдём производные $\Large u$ и $\Large v$ по $\Large x$:

$$\Large \frac{\partial u}{\partial x} = \frac{\partial}{\partial x}(xy) = y$$

$$\Large \frac{\partial v}{\partial x} = \frac{\partial}{\partial x}\left(\frac{x}{y}\right) = \frac{1}{y}$$

Подставляем:

$$\Large \frac{\partial f}{\partial x} = \frac{1}{u^3 + v^2} \cdot \left(3u^2 \cdot y + 2v \cdot \frac{1}{y}\right)$$

$$\Large = \frac{3u^2 y + \frac{2v}{y}}{u^3 + v^2}$$

Теперь подставим $\Large u = xy$ и $\Large v = \frac{x}{y}$:

$$\Large \frac{\partial f}{\partial x} = \frac{3(xy)^2 y + \frac{2 \cdot \frac{x}{y}}{y}}{(xy)^3 + \left(\frac{x}{y}\right)^2}$$

$$\Large = \frac{3x^2y^3 + \frac{2x}{y^2}}{x^3y^3 + \frac{x^2}{y^2}}$$

---

**Частная производная по $\Large y$:**

$$\Large \frac{\partial f}{\partial y} = \frac{\partial}{\partial y}\ln(u^3 + v^2)$$

Используем цепное правило:

$$\Large \frac{\partial f}{\partial y} = \frac{1}{u^3 + v^2} \cdot \frac{\partial}{\partial y}(u^3 + v^2)$$

$$\Large = \frac{1}{u^3 + v^2} \cdot \left(3u^2 \frac{\partial u}{\partial y} + 2v \frac{\partial v}{\partial y}\right)$$

Найдём производные $\Large u$ и $\Large v$ по $\Large y$:

$$\Large \frac{\partial u}{\partial y} = \frac{\partial}{\partial y}(xy) = x$$

$$\Large \frac{\partial v}{\partial y} = \frac{\partial}{\partial y}\left(\frac{x}{y}\right) = -\frac{x}{y^2}$$

Подставляем:

$$\Large \frac{\partial f}{\partial y} = \frac{1}{u^3 + v^2} \cdot \left(3u^2 \cdot x + 2v \cdot \left(-\frac{x}{y^2}\right)\right)$$

$$\Large = \frac{3u^2 x - \frac{2vx}{y^2}}{u^3 + v^2}$$

Подставим $\Large u = xy$ и $\Large v = \frac{x}{y}$:

$$\Large \frac{\partial f}{\partial y} = \frac{3(xy)^2 x - \frac{2 \cdot \frac{x}{y} \cdot x}{y^2}}{(xy)^3 + \left(\frac{x}{y}\right)^2}$$

$$\Large = \frac{3x^3y^2 - \frac{2x^2}{y^3}}{x^3y^3 + \frac{x^2}{y^2}}$$

---

**Ответ:**

$$\Large \boxed{\frac{\partial f}{\partial x} = \frac{3u^2 y + \frac{2v}{y}}{u^3 + v^2} = \frac{3x^2y^3 + \frac{2x}{y^2}}{x^3y^3 + \frac{x^2}{y^2}}}$$

$$\Large \boxed{\frac{\partial f}{\partial y} = \frac{3u^2 x - \frac{2vx}{y^2}}{u^3 + v^2} = \frac{3x^3y^2 - \frac{2x^2}{y^3}}{x^3y^3 + \frac{x^2}{y^2}}}$$
